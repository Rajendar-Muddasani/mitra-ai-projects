# Projects Reference

## Project Lanes (at mitraaiprojects.com/projects/)

### Guided Mini Projects (2–4 weeks, 2nd/3rd year students)
Quick, scoped working builds. Good for gaining confidence and adding to portfolio.

### Major Project Kits (8–16 weeks, final-year)
Thesis-ready submissions with full documentation, code, deployment, and viva prep.
**Featured: Document Q&A Assistant** — RAG system with FastAPI, OpenAI, ChromaDB. Fully available.

### Portfolio Builds (4–8 weeks, freshers)
Company-ready demos with live deployment URL. Targeted at placements and internships.

### Viva & Submission Packs
Report template, PPT template, demo script, viva defense Q&A. Works alongside any project.

## How to choose a project
- Final-year B.Tech/BCA/MCA: choose a Major Project Kit
- Fresher building portfolio: choose a Portfolio Build
- 2nd/3rd year practice: choose a Mini Project
- Already have code, need docs: choose a Viva & Submission Pack
