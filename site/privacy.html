Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026. Last updated: June 4, 2026.

1. Who We Are

mitraaiprojects.com ("the Site") is operated by Mitra AI Life, an independent education platform founded by Rajendar Muddasani. Contact: hello@mitraaiprojects.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, college/company, interest area, message
  • Account registration (via Supabase/Google OAuth): email address and basic profile
  • Course and project completion records if you sign in

Information collected automatically:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4, measurement ID G-QGY0LH6W93): page views, session data, geographic region, device type, event data (tab changes, quiz attempts, cheatsheet clicks)
  • Basic server logs (standard web server access logs)
  • Cookies set by GA4 and Supabase authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To provide course completion tracking and certificates if you sign in
  • To improve the site using aggregated analytics data
  • To understand which content is most useful to learners
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

5. Cookies

The Site uses cookies from Google Analytics (analytics tracking), Supabase (authentication session persistence), and our chat widget. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling GA4 cookies will not affect core site functionality. Disabling Supabase cookies will prevent you from staying signed in.

6. AI-Generated Content

Some images on this site are AI-generated using tools such as DALL-E 3. These are disclosed in page footers and relevant credit areas. The text content on course pages and project pages is human-authored, AI-assisted, and human-reviewed.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 2 years or until you request deletion. Course completion records are retained as long as your account exists. GA4 data is retained according to Google's standard retention settings (up to 14 months).

8. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by emailing hello@mitraaiprojects.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR. If you are in California, you have rights under CCPA.

9. Children's Privacy

This site is designed for engineering students and technical learners (typically 18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
