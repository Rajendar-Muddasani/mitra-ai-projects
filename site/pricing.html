Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free content really free? ▼
Yes, completely. All 9 courses, 57 notebooks, 9 project kits, 12 cheatsheets, and the interview prep guide are free with no sign-up required for most content. Signing in (free) just lets us track your progress and issue certificates.
When do sessions happen? ▼
Crash courses run on weekends (Saturday/Sunday). Full courses run Monday-Friday evenings (6-8 PM IST) or weekends. 1:1 sessions are booked at a mutually convenient time. All sessions are online (Google Meet or Zoom).
What if I miss a session? ▼
All sessions are recorded and shared with participants within 24 hours. For crash courses and full courses, you can attend the recording-only cohort at the same price if you cannot attend live.
Can my college group register together? ▼
Yes. Groups of 10+ from the same college get a 20% discount on crash courses and full courses. Contact us with your group size and we will send a group booking link.
Do you offer EMI or scholarships? ▼
We offer need-based discounts for students who cannot afford the fee. Contact us with your situation and we will do our best to accommodate. No one should be priced out of learning.
How is payment handled? ▼
Payment via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay after your registration interest is confirmed. We contact you to confirm the session date before any payment is requested.
